Clawbot:
>> Clawbot is a next generation AI assistant that runs on your own infrastructure (like your computer or a cloud server) and acts autonomously rather than just responding to questions.
>> Its a part of a new wave of AI agents that can execute tasks, automate workflows, and integrate with every day digital tools.

What Clawbot make different?
>> Unlike classic chatbots (e.g., ChatGPT), Clawbot doesn’t just answer prompts — it performs actions on your behalf. It is designed around principles of:
>> Autonomy – acting proactively without needing a question every time
>> Privacy & Data Control – runs on hardware you control, keeping data local
>> Execution of Real Tasks – can run shell commands, control apps, manage files, etc.
