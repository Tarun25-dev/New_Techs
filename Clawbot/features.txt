Features of Clawbot:

| Feature                         | What It Means                                    |
| ------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| **Local Deployment**            | Runs on your own machine/server                  |
| **Task Automation**             | Actively performs tasks, not just replies        |
| **Multi-Platform Messaging**    | Works across WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack, and more |
| **Extendable Skills Ecosystem** | Add plugins and integrations                     |
| **Model-Agnostic**              | Use Claude, GPT, or other models                 |
